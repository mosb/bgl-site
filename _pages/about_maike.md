Maike Osborne is Professor of Machine Learning in the Department of Engineering
Science at the University of Oxford and a Tutorial Fellow at Exeter College. She
co-directs the Oxford Martin AI Governance Initiative and leads the Bayesian
Governance Lab.

Her research spans Gaussian processes, Bayesian optimisation, and probabilistic
numerics, a field she helped found, with a growing focus on how a principled
treatment of uncertainty can make AI systems trustworthy and governable. She is
also co-founder and Chief Science Officer of [Mind Foundry](https://www.mindfoundry.ai),
an Oxford spin-out.
