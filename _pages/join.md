The lab is deliberately small. We would rather take on a few researchers we can work with closely than build a large group, so joining us is less about filling a post than about finding a genuine overlap of problems and temperament.

We welcome enquiries from prospective DPhil students, postdoctoral researchers, and collaborators whose interests sit close to probabilistic machine learning and the governance of AI. 
There are no formally advertised vacancies at present, but a good fit can make its own opportunity. If you would like to discuss possibilities, please write to [maike.osborne@eng.ox.ac.uk](mailto:maike.osborne@eng.ox.ac.uk) with a short note on how your interests intersect with ours and a CV.

**Prospective DPhil students** should also consult the [AIMS Centre for Doctoral Training](https://aims.robots.ox.ac.uk) and the Department of Engineering Science admissions pages for studentships and deadlines.
